Web Escape

Web Escape is an online interactive game built for the gaming community in mind. Work in a team or go solo on our fun and exciting adventures. Your goal is to escape all the rooms in your game to win prizes and money. Use this money to buy weapons, clothing, and more. The more in your party, the harder the rooms get. If you're every stuck, feel free to talk to the live chat forum where thousands of gamers are online answering questions about the rooms, giving hints and advice, and talking for fun. The fun is limitless, start your journey now!
